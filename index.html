Our mission is to make forever chemicals (PFAS) obsolete.

We start by making textile finishes fully plastic free, without compromising performance. No trade-offs.

Your clothes are dripping in forever chemicals

We make water-proof and oil-proof coatings from renewable organic sources, free from forever chemicals (PFAS) and free from synthetic plastics (polyurethanes).

We upcycle agricultural waste to valuable textile finishes. Our processing is low in water and energy. 

Our new coatings are

Plastic free

Our technology, based on biopolymers and inorganic minerals, uses natural agricultural waste. No synthetic plastics. No bio-based monomers.

Plug & Play

Our technology is compatible with already scalable manufacturing processes. It has been optimised for cellulose and protein-based fibres.

Omniphobic

Our nanotechnology-based coating can repel water (biomimetic to the lotus effect) and in principle can be adapted to repel oil-based liquids.
